> _Or what I talk about when I talk about music_

At some point around 2016 I ran into this [youtube channel](https://www.youtube.com/c/RicardoMara%C3%B1a). It kick started my love for KPM Music Recorded Library, for Italian masters in Umilane Morricone, Piccioni, and Umilani.

At some point around 2017 I ran into [this blog](https://lampnoakari.jugem.cc/#gsc.tab=0). It kick started my love for MPB and Bossa Nova, to Nacimento, Borges, Valle, Costa, and Veloso, to Soul, Funk, to Wonder, Mayfield, Matsutoya, Otis, and Blunsfield/

At some point around 2022 I ran into [this publication](). It kick started my love for contemporary post rock, deepened my love for ambient and drone and the noise, in Boris,

At some point last year I ran into [this account](https://x.com/geordiegreep?lang=en). It kick started my love for ECM Jazz, for folk classics like Joni Mitchell and Laura Nyro, etc. etc.

Every year I think that it doesn't get better than this and every year I prove myself wrong. I want to hold on to these memories, as if

We have to talk about where to find it — in real life in record stores, at your favourite cafe, on your communal elevator, online on your favourite record label's discogs page, on your favourite artist's whosampled page, on a collaborative playlist, on a mixtape burned into a cd by the one that got away, on your mum's old collection of cassettes on your grandpa's favourite vinyl, on a collaborative playlist, a blend, a blog post, on a reply to `source pls?` comment, in an essay by Ted or Chuck or

We have to talk about the people behind them — they're related to who? She was in a cabin by herself? Why was he at that ? by What's in who's bag and why aren't they in my closet? He's got issues like I've got issues and trust me I've got issues.

Give me everything but don't actually give me the science, the theory, or the sheet music, because if I can't talk about it with my neighbours then what's the point?

Music is one of life's last mysteries what makes a good melody? What makes a good sound? Even if you know what makes a good sound _why_ does a good sound sound good and how does that make you feel?
